About me

I started my adventure with graphic design programs as a child. From MS Paint, to Adobe Photoshop, I developed my knowledge and graphic skills by creating images for a blog, which also sparked my interest in HTML and CSS.

When I was choosing what to study at university, I was initially driven by the desire to create websites, which led me to choose a degree in Computer Science. When I finished, I decided to continue studying at the Faculty of Fine Arts to broaden my horizons to include graphic design as well.

At the moment, my job combines both fields of study by creating graphics and dealing with websites. As a perfectionist, I think I still have a lot to learn, but my love of learning and development and my willingness to take on challenges help me along the way.

What do I do?

In my current job, I am primarily a graphic designer, creating a variety of marketing materials, including banners, posters, calendars, catalogues, flyers, and more, for both print and online publication. I am also experienced in retouching and photo editing, creating illustrations, developing corporate identities, and designing websites. Lately, I have become interested in user experience (UX) design and am actively seeking to develop my skills in this area.

On a side, I am also responsible for taking care of a couple of websites using the Joomla system and, more recently, Wordpress. Frequently, I also check in at the print shop, where I operate a printing and a cutting plotter.

What am I looking for?

A new place where I can spread my wings even further as a graphic designer. I love learning new things, so challenges do not scare me :) I am willing to work on a permanent basis on an employment contract or occasionally on a freelance basis.
